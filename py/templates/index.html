You can AND keywords with spaces: keyword1 keyword2

You can OR keywords with OR: keyword1 OR keyword2

You can NOT keywords with a dash: -keyword

You can group keywords with parentheses: (happy OR party) (holiday OR house) -(birthday OR democratic OR republican)

You can exact match with backslash quotation marks: \"keyword1 keyword2\"

You can substring match with contains: contains:keyword

How to Use

Click the plus button on the top right to create a new GNIP job (i.e. to retrieve historic Twitter data based on a new set of location, timeframe, and keywords).

Click on a job to the left to get more info, or to accept or reject if the job is quoted, or to download the associated Twitter data files if the job is at 100%.
